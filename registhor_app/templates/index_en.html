Course Codes

To get the course codes that match the filters' criteria, use route:

/api/v1/comments/course-codes/question_type?key=YOUR_API_KEY&department_code=CES[&fiscal_year=1960-61]

Accepted values for 'question_type':

  • general
  • improvement
  • technical

Note: Fiscal year in standard TBS hyphenated format.


Counts

To get the number of comments a course has received by stars, use route:

/api/v1/comments/counts/question_type?key=YOUR_API_KEY&department_code=CES[&course_code=D101][&fiscal_year=1960-61]

Accepted values for 'question_type':

  • general
  • improvement
  • technical

Note: Fiscal year in standard TBS hyphenated format.


Text

To get the raw text and attributes of comments a course has received, use route:

/api/v1/comments/text/question_type?key=YOUR_API_KEY&department_code=CES&limit=20[&course_code=D101][&fiscal_year=1960-61][&stars=5][&offset=0][&lang=fr]

Accepted values for 'question_type':

  • general
  • improvement
  • technical

Note: Accepted values for 'stars' are integers 1 through 5, inclusively.

Note: Fiscal year in standard TBS hyphenated format.


Mandatory Courses

To get all active courses and whether a department considers them mandatory for its employees, use route:

/api/v1/departments/mandatory-courses?key=YOUR_API_KEY&department_code=CES

Note: This route also accepts methods DELETE and POST when accompanied by a JSON object of format {"department_code":"CES","course_code":"D101"}.

Counts by City

To get the number of offerings by city taking place on a given date, or within a date range, use route:

/api/v1/offerings/counts-by-city?key=YOUR_API_KEY&date_1=1960-01-01[&date_2=1960-01-03][&business_line=Digital Academy][&clients_only=true][&course_code=D101][&exclude_cancelled=true][&instructor_name=Paula][&lang=fr]

Note: Dates in standard YYYY-MM-DD ISO format.

Note: Parameters 'clients_only' and 'exclude_cancelled' are boolean.


Offering Information

To get all offerings taking place on a given date, or within a date range, use route:

/api/v1/offerings/offering-information?key=YOUR_API_KEY&date_1=1960-01-01[&date_2=1960-01-03][&business_line=Digital Academy][&clients_only=true][&course_code=D101][&exclude_cancelled=true][&instructor_name=Paula][&limit=20][&offset=0][&lang=fr]

Note: Dates in standard YYYY-MM-DD ISO format.

Note: Parameters 'clients_only' and 'exclude_cancelled' are boolean.
